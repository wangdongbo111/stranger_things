The Story So Far

Set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, the first season focuses on the investigation into the disappearance of a young boy (Will Byers) amid supernatural events occurring around the town, including the appearance of a girl with psychokinetic abilities (Eleven).

The Lore

The Mind Flayer

A malevolent entity that rules the Upside Down. It possesses a hive mind, controlling all organisms within its dimension. Its ultimate goal is to consume and conquer other worlds.

The Gate

A tear in the fabric of reality connecting Hawkins to the Upside Down. While the initial gate was opened psychically by Eleven, machines and other methods have been used to reopen it.
